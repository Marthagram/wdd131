Our Purpose

Agriculture is key in creating a healthy Nation. And so, it is of great importance that we encourage our farmers and support them through:

  • Government yearly palliatives for Agriculture
  • creating opportunities for exportation of farmers' goods
  • sensitization, coaching and mentoring through sharing ideas within farmers inorder to boost the Nation's Agriculture with happy farmers.
